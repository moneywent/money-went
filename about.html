Our Mission

Do you know where your money went? Here at MoneyWent we believe in the importance of knowing how your tax money was put into use. We subscribe to the notion that a well informed population is the key to a successful democracy and allows voters to make informed decisions. The federal budget is constatnly changing and is often difficult to understand. Our goal at MoneyWent is to use our expertise and sources to allow you to easily understand where exactly your money went.

Currently we only offer a calculator for United States Federal taxes, but we are working hard to expand into US state taxes and other countries. We created MoneyWent out of a passion for informing people, and the amount of money we generate from displaying ads doesn't cover the costs of our servers. If, like us, you believe in the vision of MoneyWent and would like to support future development and maintance of the website, please consider donating to our cause using the PayPal Donate button below or, if you would prefer, we will have a coffee on you by supporting us with Ko-Fi.
